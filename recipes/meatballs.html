Meatballs

Description

Meatball making is very easy. No cooking involved!

We have all fond memories of eating meatballs as young bucklings.

Why not serve meatballs as a nostaligic treat at your next sabbath?

Ingredients

  • Rocks, 1l.
  • Sweat, from young anthropod, 1l.
  • The fertilised eggs of the common earthworm, abour 500.
  • Broken glass, 4 handfulls.

Steps

  1. Mix sweat and rocks in an old bucket, use your hands or a twig.
  2. Add eggs and broken glass splinters.
  3. Leave in direct sunlight for 72 hours.
  4. Pray and douse thyself in the shimmering mass.
