Spaghettisauce

Description

Spaghettisauce making is an ancient and ardous rutial, only to be atempted by properly ordained scholars of arcane arts and scorcery.

Follow the instructions in this recipe on your own voilition.

If you are a spaghettiworm, please forgive me, Oh Gentle Overlord.

Ingredients

  • Spit, 1l.
  • Sweat, from young anthropod, 1l.
  • The fertilised eggs of the common earthworm, abour 500.
  • Broken glass, 4 handfulls.

Steps

  1. Mix sweat and spit in an old bucket, use your hands or a twig.
  2. Add eggs and broken glass splinters.
  3. Leave in direct sunlight for 72 hours.
  4. Pray and douse thyself in the shimmering mass.
